Welcome

It is the mission of the Wichita Grizzlies Non-Profit Youth Foundation to develop and empower today’s youth with confidence, determination, accountability and integrity, so they can live a productive and prosperous tomorrow. Our goal is to teach young athletes respect for themselves, their peers and their community by instilling a code of ethics built on integrity, responsibility and teamwork.

Together, our players, families, and volunteers work with unyielding commitment to cultivate a safe and disciplined environment so every child can reach their full potential, develop their talent, and increase their skills on and off the field and more importantly… life.
