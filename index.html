Welcome

The Wichita Grizzlies youth football program is dedicated to the development of teamwork, work ethic, and sportsmanship of each and every player. Players are expected to give maximal effort throughout a rigorous practice regiment, follow the instructions of the coaching staff readily, and display utmost respect for coaches, parents, officials, and fellow players. Adhering to firm principles is what has allowed the Wichita Grizzlies to achieve competative success and earn the strong and loyal support of the team parents.
